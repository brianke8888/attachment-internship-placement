Features

Everything you need for placements

From profile creation to final placement, PlacementHub handles the entire workflow.

Student Profiles

Create a detailed profile with your course, skills, and uploaded CV. Stand out to potential employers.

Browse & Apply

Search internships by category, location, and deadline. Apply with one click and track your applications.

Company Postings

Companies post internship opportunities, review applicants, and shortlist candidates with ease.

Application Tracking

Real-time status updates from pending to reviewed, shortlisted, accepted, or rejected — all in one place.

Real-time Notifications

Get instant push notifications when your application status changes or a new student applies.

Admin Dashboard

Full platform oversight with user management, placement tools, and CSV report exports.
